\section*{Simple Table Example}

Here is a basic table in LaTeX:

\begin{table}[h]  % 'h' means place the table here
\centering       % centers the table
\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|}  % 3 columns, all centered, with vertical lines
\hline                   % horizontal line at the top
Name & Age & City \\      % column headers
\hline
Alice & 25 & London \\    % first row
Bob & 30 & Paris \\       % second row
Charlie & 22 & New York \\% third row
\hline                   % horizontal line at the bottom
\end{tabular}
\caption{Example of a simple table}
\label{tab:example}
\end{table}
